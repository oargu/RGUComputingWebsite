How to Apply

If you are interested in an undergraduate course, apply online via UCAS (Universities and Colleges Admissions Service) for admission. Applications for most Postgraduate courses are made direct to the University.

After you applyStep 1 Choose a subject area that interests you from the Subjects pages or from the Home Page.

Step 2 Find out how to apply from within the course information.

Step 3 Apply via UCAS (most undergraduate courses) or via Apply Online.
